Alberto Manguel (born 1948 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentine-born writer, translator, and editor. He is the author of numerous non-fiction books such as The Dictionary of Imaginary Places (in 1980) and A History of Reading (1996) The Library at Night (2007), and novels such as News From a Foreign Country Came (1991).
